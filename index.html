About the Initiative

Our Ask

The Varsity Rugby Initiative proposes a two-year sanctioning pilot with the Montana High School Association — bringing sanctioned rugby to Montana high schools starting Fall 2028.

Demand Already Exists

Montana athletes want to play, with nowhere sanctioned to do it.

Low Cost to Schools

Shared fields, minimal equipment, a spring season that avoids conflicts.

Fully Supported

Backed by USA Rugby standards, insurance, and an active club network.

Montana is the fastest-growing rugby state in the country. Two nationally recognized tournaments — the Montana Invitational and the Great Northwest Challenge — already showcase the state's talent, and the NCAA recognizes women's rugby as an Emerging Sport, giving Montana athletes a real path to college competition.

Easy to Start

How Easy It Is to Play

Rugby asks less of a school than almost any other varsity sport — a fraction of the cost, with no new facility required. A spring season lets it share football and soccer fields without competing for space or athletes in the fall and winter.

What Rugby Needs

  • Balls, cones & mouthguards
  • A shared grass field
  • A USA Rugby–certified coach
  • USA Rugby player insurance

What It Doesn't

  • No helmets or pads
  • No dedicated stadium
  • No specialized turf
  • No major capital outlay

Program Details

How the Pilot Would Work

Format

A spring season of small-sided Rugby Sevens to start — minimizing facility and schedule conflicts.

Governance

Sanctioned by MHSA and administered to USA Rugby standards, with national-body insurance.

Coaching & Safety

All coaches USA Rugby–certified, with mandatory concussion and safe-contact training.

Participation

Open to all classifications, with separate girls' and boys' divisions where numbers allow.

Proposed Timeline

A Measured, Reversible Path

  1. 2026–27 Board approval & joint exploratory committee
  2. 2027–28 Coach certification & school onboarding
  3. Fall 2028 Pilot Year 1 launches
  4. 2029–30 Pilot Year 2 & data collection
  5. 2030 Review & full-sanctioning decision

Get Involved

The Ask

We're asking the MHSA to approve a two-year sanctioning pilot for high school rugby, beginning Fall 2028 — starting with a joint exploratory committee, then finalized safety standards, then confirmed participating schools ahead of launch.
